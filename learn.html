♟️ Meet the Pieces

Each piece moves in a special way. Let’s meet them:

  • Pawn : Moves forward one square, captures diagonally.
  • Rook: Moves in straight lines — up, down, and sideways.
  • Knight: Jumps in an L-shape. It’s the only piece that can jump!
  • Bishop: Glides diagonally across the board.
  • Queen: The most powerful — moves in any direction!
  • King: Moves one square in any direction. Protect him!
