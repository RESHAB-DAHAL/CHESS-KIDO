KING

The king moves one square in any direction and is the most important piece; checkmate ends the game. King safety is a primary strategic concern.

Key Characteristics:

Movement One square in any direction; essential to keep safe early and active in endgame.
Castle Castling improves safety and activates a rook.

Strategic Importance:

  • Safety Early king safety is vital; castling is a common tool.
  • Endgame King becomes an attacking piece in pawn endgames.
  • Tactics Exposed kings are vulnerable to mating nets and tactical shots.

Proper king play balances safety with timely activation as the game simplifies.
